Bespoke

Generate tailored CVs and cover letters with local AI — directly in your browser.

What is Bespoke?

Bespoke is a browser extension that reads a job listing, combines it with your saved profile, and uses a locally-running AI model (via Ollama) to generate a customised CV and cover letter — all without sending your data to third-party cloud services.

Key Features

  • One-click CV & cover letter generation from any job page
  • Works with local Ollama models — your data stays on your machine
  • Optional Google Drive backup for your profile
  • Supports Chrome and Firefox
View on GitHub

Privacy

Bespoke stores your profile locally on your device and only contacts the AI endpoint you configure. No analytics, no tracking, no data sold to third parties.
